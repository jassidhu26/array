University Fee Structure

Tuition Fee

The tuition fee varies based on the program of study. Below are the details for some of our popular programs:

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science: Rs 1200000 per year
  • Bachelor of Arts in Economics: Rs 11,00000 per year
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA): Rs10,00000 per year

Payment Methods: Gpay,Ptm,Debit card/Credit card


cash and cheque are not acceptable

  • Installment Plan: Three installments due in August, December, and April
